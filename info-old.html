Sam is an independent designer, art director, and creative consultant helping organizations tell their complex stories in approachable ways. 
‍
He's spent the last 15 years creating brand identities, design systems, magazines, apps, websites, and more for clients ranging from yoga studios to international airlines. He helped create the Genome brand and magazine, helped redesign foreignaffairs.com and afar.com, and launched countless apps and products. His work has been recognized by the Society of Publication Designers, Communication Arts, Print, and Graphis, and was selected to sit on the design jury for the SPD annual design competition.
